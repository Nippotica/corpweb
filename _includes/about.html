About Nippotica

At Nippotica, we are committed to creating a generation of science-based ventures, with a focus on deeptech innovation. Our mission is to strategically seed, nurture, and propel startups from inception through their growth trajectory.

We provide a comprehensive ecosystem that equips entrepreneurs with the essential tools and connections to thrive. This includes not just funding and strategic guidance but also access to top-tier talent and a robust support network.

Our platform is designed to embrace a diverse range of entrepreneurs, enabling them to excel in their respective markets.

Through our extensive global network of industry experts and collaborations with leading research institutions, we ensure that our startups remain at the forefront of deeptech innovation, navigating the complexities of their markets with confidence.

We invite you to visit the websites of our two 2024 ventures: Nippofin, specializing in fast financial computing, and Algotechniq, delivering industrial AI solutions.
